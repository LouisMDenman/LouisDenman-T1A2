ABOUT ME

As you probably saw from the home page of this website, my name's Louis Denman. I'm an aspiring web development student, currently learning through the Coder Academy's 10 month bootcamp, and have made this portfolio as an intial project to demonstrate the skills that I have aquired through the course. I've always had a personal interest with all things technology, and when I was introduced to coding in my year 7 Information Technology course, I knew it was something that I thoroughly enjoyed and wanted to pursue. For more information about my previous coding history, as well as aspirations in the future, see the my coding journey section. Otherwise, feel free to have a look below for a few personal interests that I enjoy when I'm not coding!

Moutain Biking

Although I definitely need to get on the bike more than I have recently, I still really enjoy mountain biking. I've been riding since 2014, when I was only 12 years of age on my old hardtail. When I started taking it a bit more seriously, I saved up and got myself a Giant Stance, which was my first dual suspension bike. On this bike I participated in local competitions, and always had a great time riding it. In 2017, I upgraded to a Trek Remedy 7, seeing as when I got older I was more interested in hitting bigger jumps and harder trails. That same year, however, I went over the handlebars when I came short on a notoriously large local gap, and managed to snap my collarbone and completely disrupt my AC joint. Luckily I made a full recovery, but since then I have taken it a bit easier, and been lucky enough to ride some awesome spots in Tasmania and New Zealand.

Basketball

Later in high school I took an interest in basketball; I had mates who were interested and played it and I was frequently watching highlight clips from games and liked shooting the ball around at recess. As a result I signed up to play in 2018 at school, and played 2 years of school and then 2 years of college. Standing at a resoudingly average height, I wasn't too skilled or built for it but always had a good time playing with friends.

Soccer

Soccer has been my longest standing hobby, seeing as I started playing in 2009. I was most serious in high school, commiting to club and school games, participating in preseason and postseason camps to be on top of my game. Through these ventures, I had the pleasure of playing soccer next to some great talent of which are now in Germany, Spain and the A-League, as well as meet some friends who I am still close with to this day. After my college season in 2021 I have not played, but I am inteding on starting to play again for a local club next season.

Car Enthusiast

Growing up I had a soft spot for all things subaru, mainly BRZs and Hawkeye WRXs, but as I've grown up to this point I have come to appreciate most enthusiast vehicles and styles including, JDMs, Euros, American and Australian muscle. After saving up some money towards the end of my P plate lisence, I wanted something fun that was in my budget, and settled on my current car that is pictured below: A subaru liberty. Whilst not quite a WRX, this one is a 3.0R Spec B, which came with a similar 6 speed manual transmission and a unique boxer 6 engine.
